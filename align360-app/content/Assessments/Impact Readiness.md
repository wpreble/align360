# Impact Readiness

*20 questions · about 7 minutes · Single choice · Your Conviction Score across five domains: Identity, Capability, Rejection, Direction, Belonging*

## Identity

### Q1 — Self-Perception

> You walk into a room of highly accomplished people, founders, executives, leaders whose work you genuinely admire. Before a word is spoken, what happens internally?

- **A.** I assume I have little to offer and stay quiet. → 0
- **B.** I feel the comparison pull and wonder if I belong. → 3
- **C.** I listen carefully and look for where I can genuinely contribute. → 7
- **D.** I engage naturally. I know what I bring, even in a room like this. → 10

### Q2 — Validation Dependency

> You complete something significant, a project, a decision, a creative work. Before anyone has responded, how do you feel about it?

- **A.** Uncertain, I cannot assess its value until others respond. → 0
- **B.** Anxious, I need external feedback before I can feel settled. → 3
- **C.** Reasonably confident, but genuinely curious about others' perspectives. → 7
- **D.** Clear, I have my own internal standard and I know whether I met it. → 10

### Q3 — AI-Era Stability

> AI is rapidly automating work that once required years of expertise. Someone tells you your core skill set will be significantly less differentiated in three years. Your honest first response is:

- **A.** I feel threatened and question whether my value holds. → 0
- **B.** I worry but am not sure what to do with that information. → 3
- **C.** I evaluate what is genuinely mine that AI cannot replicate. → 7
- **D.** I already know my irreplaceable edge. Disruption confirms it, not threatens it. → 10

### Q4 — Core Clarity

> Someone asks you to describe who you are, not what you do, in a way that would be true regardless of your title or current circumstances. How does that feel?

- **A.** I struggle significantly. I define myself largely by role or output. → 0
- **B.** I can describe myself but it feels uncertain or context-dependent. → 3
- **C.** I have a working answer that feels mostly true and reasonably stable. → 7
- **D.** I answer clearly and consistently. Who I am doesn't change with circumstances. → 10
