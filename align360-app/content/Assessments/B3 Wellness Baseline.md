# B3 Wellness — Baseline Assessment

*18 questions  ·  ~5 minutes  ·  Score 1–5  ·  Answer for today only, not your average*

Design note:  B3 baseline is taken once to establish a physiological profile. It is NOT a personality test. Scores are 1 (depleted) to 5 (optimal) per question. Domain averages determine the day type: A Day (4.2+), B Day (3.0–4.1), C Day (<3.0). Sleep (Q12) and decision confidence (Q18) have override logic — see scoring reference at end of section.

Design note:  Answer instruction shown to user: 'Answer based on today only — not your best day, not your worst. Just right now. The most useful answer is the honest one.'
