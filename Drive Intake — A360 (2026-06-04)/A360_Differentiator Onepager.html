Data architecture · The semantic bridge

Individual data stays personal. Meaning informs the organization.

This is the most important architectural decision Align360 has made — and it is the one enterprise buyers will ask about first. The answer is simple and defensible: we do not build a data bridge. We build a semantic bridge.

How it works
Unidirectional data flow. Two-directional meaning.
External sources — APIs, calendar, tools, enterprise signals — flow in to improve the User Model. Nothing flows out. On the enterprise side, individual raw data never crosses to the org layer. Only aggregated, anonymized semantic signals cross — what the pattern means for the organization, never what any individual is experiencing.
